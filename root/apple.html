iPHONE 12 PRO

The iPhone 12 Pro is one of most feature-rich iPhones you can buy right now.
It has the greatest of all of Apple's features inside, and it's certainly the model to consider if you're questioning whether the iPhone 12 has enough raw power for you.
Announced alongside the iPhone 12, iPhone 12 mini and iPhone 12 Pro Max, the key upgrades over the iPhone 12 are subtle:

  • There's slightly more RAM, but the same chipset inside.
  • There are three camera sensors on the rear, not two, so you can now get closer to subjects and scenes with 2x optical zoom.
